Game Description
Achromatic Tale is a psychological horror visual novel that explores emotional intelligence, decision-making, and the delicate balance between light and darkness.
You wake up in a mystical forest, greeted by small, mushroom-like creatures called the Shroomies. Soon, you encounter Bjork, an enigmatic and unpredictable being whose emotions shape the world around him. His mood swings transform the once-vibrant landscape into something eerie and hostile.
Your dialogue choices directly impact Bjork‚Äôs temperament‚Äîwill you soothe his troubled soul or push him further into madness? As you navigate shifting realities, you must uncover the secrets of Bjork‚Äôs past, the curse that binds him, and the ancient script that only the cursed can see.
With multiple endings, including a tragic demise, survival, or redemption, Achromatic Tale challenges players to make meaningful choices in a world that reacts to every decision.

The History of Achromatic Tale

Developed by Twisted Realm Studio

The Birth of an Idea

"Achromatic Tale" began as a passion project by our small indie team, Twisted Realm Studio. Our goal was simple: to create a visual novel that blends horror, fantasy, and psychological tension while giving players emotional choices that truly impact the game world.

Inspired by dark fairy tales, psychological horror, and games that explore morality, we wanted to create a world that reacts dynamically to player choices. From the start, we knew this wouldn't be a traditional visual novel‚Äîit had to be something more immersive, something that left players questioning their own decisions.

Development Process

Development started just a week ago, making this one of our fastest yet most intense game jams. Our team split into different roles:

  • Story & Dialogue - Crafted to immerse the player in a surreal, eerie world.
  • Art & Character Design - Every sprite and background was hand-drawn to maintain a distinct, eerie aesthetic.
  • Coding & Mechanics -Built using Ren'Py for the game and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the website.
  • Music & Sound Design - Every sound effect and background track was chosen to enhance the shifting moods of the game.

Despite the tight deadline, our shared vision kept us pushing forward. We encountered obstacles‚Äîdebugging choice-based mechanics, adjusting Bjork's transformations, and ensuring the mood shifts were seamless‚Äîbut each challenge made the game stronger.

The Core Concept

In "Achromatic Tale", the player wakes up in a mysterious fairytale-like forest inhabited by small creatures called Shroomies. However, the real mystery begins when they meet Bjork, a mysterious and unpredictable creature whose emotions physically alter the world around him.

Every dialogue choice directly influences Bjork's emotional state, causing the once-bright and whimsical forest to either remain safe or transform into a nightmarish realm. As the player delves deeper, they must unravel the dark past of this world, navigate Bjork's shifting moods, and ultimately determine whether the realm is saved or doomed.

Innovative Features

  • Emotionally reactive AI - Bjork's personality shifts dynamically based on the player's choices.
  • Multiple Endings - Players can reach vastly different conclusions depending on their relationship with Bjork.
  • Hand-Drawn Art - Every visual element was created from scratch for a unique atmosphere.
  • Mini-Games & Quick Battles - Unlike most visual novels, "Achromatic Tale" includes interactive elements like duels and survival moments.
  • Dark Psychological Themes - The story challenges players to consider morality, empathy, and how emotions shape reality.

What's Next?

"Achromatic Tale" was created as a school project, developed within a short time frame for our upcoming presentation on Thursday. While we don't plan to continue development beyond this version, the process has been an incredible learning experience for our team.

We're excited to showcase our work, share our creative journey, and see how players react to Bjork's story. This project challenged us to think critically, collaborate effectively, and push our creative limits‚Äîmaking it an experience we won't forget.
